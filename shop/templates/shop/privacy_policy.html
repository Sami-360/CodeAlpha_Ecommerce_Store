Privacy Policy

Ecommerce Store collects only the information needed to provide account, checkout, order tracking, and support features. This may include your name, email address, saved addresses, cart activity, wishlist items, and order history.

We use customer information to process orders, calculate checkout totals, maintain account preferences, respond to contact messages, and improve the shopping experience. We do not sell personal information to third parties.

Payment handling, shipping, and analytics integrations in a production environment should follow their own privacy and security standards. Store data should be managed according to the privacy and retention policies used by the business.

You may update your profile and address book from your account page. For privacy questions, contact the store team through the contact form.
